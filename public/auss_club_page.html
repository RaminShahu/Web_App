Adelaide University Space Society

About

The Adelaide University Space Society is a club for anyone with a passion for space, no matter what you study. We exist to create a community of space enthusiasts, and connect students to the space industry. We do this through our many fantastic events, with a variety of fun, social, and professional events across the year. We want to capture your passion for space and show you how that passion can lead to incredible careers.

Did we mention that registration is FREE!!!

Members get a discount on select events, as well as access to our merch! If you would like to register, please fill in the form below.

A massive thank you to our industry partners who make it possible for us to deliver such high quality events without charging a membership fee. They are:

KPMG, ELA, NOVA Systems

AWS, Silentium Defence, SASIC
