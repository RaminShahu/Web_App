Data Science In The Real World

About

Enough talk about theory! Have you ever wondered how Data Science is actually used in the real world?

Join us on Wednesday, June 24th at the much-anticipated event “Data Science in the real world” as we delve into the exciting realm of data science and its profound impact on our everyday lives with our esteemed guest Jake Prajer, a renowned Senior Analyst from Deloitte. With years of experience and a wealth of knowledge in the field, Jake is here to share his insights, expertise, and real-world examples that will leave you inspired and amazed.

But it doesn't end there! Following the presentation, we have an exciting Q&A session where you'll have the opportunity to interact directly with Jake. This is your exclusive chance to gain valuable advice, dive deeper into, and explore your potential career in the world of data science!
